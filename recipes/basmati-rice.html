Basmati Rice

Description

Basmati rice is a type of rice enjoyed around the world. It is a long grain rice with a nutty flavor and a pleasant aroma. Cooking it is easy!

This recipe is very straightforward. Basmati rice can be customized, but this recipe will only include the basic steps. We will be using white basmati rice for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 3 Cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • A teaspoon of whole cumin

Steps

  1. Wash the basmati rice 3 to 4 times. Drain the water to remove excess starch and dirt.
  2. Soak the basmati rice for at least 30 minutes for fluffier rice.
  3. Bring the water to a boil, adding in the salt and cumin seeds.
  4. When the water boils, add in the rice and lemon juice.
  5. Cook with the lid partially covered until all the water has been absorbed or evaporated.
  6. Cover the rice with the lid and let steam for at least 15 minutes.
  7. After 15 minutes, remove lid and fluff rice.
  8. Optionally, add olive oil before serving.
