Raita

Description

Raita is a popular side dish from South Asian cuisine. It is enjoyed with a variety of spicy and flavorful dishes because of its smooth texture and cooling, tart taste. Like many South Asian dishes, raita can be customized for your tastes and needs, or whatever you have on hand!

This recipe is a simple raita with a few ingredients.

Ingredients

For the base:

  • 1/2 cup of curd or yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Optional ingredients:

  • Grated cucumber
  • Chopped parseley
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Diced tomato
  • Diced red onion

The optional ingredients can be added in any amount. I didn't include any quantity because, really, it is pretty much just eyeballing and seeing what you like or don't. Even the base raita by itself is so tasty,

Steps

  1. Mix the yogurt and the water.
  2. Add in the spices.
  3. Mix to combine.
  4. Mix in optional ingredients. You can use only one of them or all of them.
  5. Serve chilled.
